About this Project:

Hi this is a project my sister and me started a few months back.
Short Story:
Basically the Instacarters would give us
1. Bad Produce,
2. Bad Replacements,
3. Orders don't get picked up unless you tip well. (expensive as heck)
So why not make something that fixes all these issues :).
I also worked as an InstaCarter and my experience kind of sucked too.
1. The pay isn't great,
2. the app sometimes wouldn't even work,
3. dealing with different customers each time can be fustrating as a shopper (don't know what customer wants)

How about a service that grocery shoppers are going to catter to the needs of the customer.
So Cartful was created. :)
